Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18th December, 2025

Introduction

The 20 Minute App ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application operates and how we handle your information.

Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The 20 Minute App operates entirely locally on your device. We do not maintain any servers, and we do not transmit any of your usage data, app selection preferences, or screen time statistics to any external parties.

Types of Data Produced

  • App Usage Stats: All usage statistics are retrieved directly from your device's system services and are displayed only to you within the app.
  • Preferences: Your settings (such as selected apps to monitor or time limits) are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions to function correctly:

  • Usage Access: Required to monitor the time you spend on selected apps.
  • Notification Permission: Required to display persistent notifications for the foreground service.
  • Display over other apps: To launch the 20 Minute App from the background when your time limit is reached.

These permissions are used solely for the core functionality of the app and not for data collection or tracking.
